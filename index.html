frequently asked questions

Do I need a referral to see a physiotherapist?

No, you can book directly. We also work with your doctor to coordinate care if needed.

What should I wear to my appointment?

Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows movement. For lower body issues, shorts are helpful.

How long is a typical session?

Initial assessments are 45-60 minutes. Follow-up sessions are usually 30-45 minutes.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we are empaneled with most major insurance providers. Please check with us for your specific plan.

What techniques do you use?

We combine manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, dry needling, and electrotherapy based on your needs.

Will my sessions be covered by insurance?

We provide all necessary documentation for reimbursement. Coverage depends on your policy.
